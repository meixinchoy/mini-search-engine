Billion is a name for a large number. It may refer specifically to: 1,000,000,000 (109, one thousand million), the short scale definition now normal in both British and American English 1,000,000,000,000 (1012, one million million), the long scale definition used formerly in Britain and currently in certain other languages Billion may also refer to: Billions (TV series), a Showtime series Billions (film), a 1920 silent comedy Billion (company), a Taiwanese modem manufacturer Jack Billion (born 1939), the 2006 Democratic Party candidate for governor of South Dakota Mr. Billion, a 1977 film by Jonathan Kaplan
